F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Mostly asked questions from our clients include:

What is the difference between retained search and contingency recruiting?

Retained search is focused on finding high-impact leaders for critical roles. Contingency recruiting typically focuses on active job seekers rather than passive executive talent.

How do you ensure cultural alignment and leadership

We evaluate candidates using behavioral interviews and evidence-based assessments to ensure their leadership style aligns with your organization’s unique culture and ecosystem.

How long does a typical executive search take?

A standard search typically takes 40 days from initial brief to offer. Our partner-led process includes four key phases: market mapping and research, candidate engagement and screening, finalist interviews, and final negotiation/onboarding support.
